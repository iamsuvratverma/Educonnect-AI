About ASL

Bridging Communication with Sign Language to Text

Our Sign Language to Text Conversion tool bridges communication gaps by translating American Sign Language (ASL) gestures into written text in real-time. This innovative feature empowers individuals who use ASL to communicate more effectively in educational and professional settings. By converting gestures into readable text, our tool ensures that Deaf and hard-of-hearing individuals have equal access to information and can participate fully in conversations and learning environments. This project is a step forward in making communication more inclusive and accessible for everyone.
